Lucas Weekly Fencing Training & Recovery Plan / Lucas 每周花剑训练与恢复计划

Goal: improve morning readiness, late-bout stamina, recovery quality, and avoid overtraining.
目标：提升晨间竞技启动、比赛后半程体能、恢复质量，并避免过度训练。
Core principle: morning work is for neural activation, not exhaustion. If legs feel heavy, explosiveness drops, or focus declines, reduce morning load first. / 核心原则：早晨训练以神经激活为主，不追求疲劳；如果出现持续腿沉、爆发下降、注意力差，应先减少晨间刺激量。

Competition Week Rules / 比赛周额外规则

Local Early Competition / 本地早场比赛

  • If competition starts at 8:00, wake up at 5:45-6:00.如果 8:00 开始，比赛日 5:45-6:00 起床。
  • Do dynamic warm-up, quick feet, and short explosive work.必须做动态热身、Quick Feet、短爆发。
  • Do not warm up slowly or casually.禁止慢悠悠热身。

Texas Competition / 德州比赛

  • 8:00 AM in Texas feels like 6:00 AM in California.德州 8:00 AM 相当于加州 6:00 AM。
  • Starting 4 days before travel, move bedtime and wake-up 20-30 minutes earlier each day.出发前 4 天开始，每天提前 20-30 分钟睡觉和起床。
  • Do not shift the schedule by 2 hours all at once.不要一次性调整 2 小时。
Weekly Monitoring / 每周监控指标

What to Watch / 观察内容

  • Late-session performance during Wednesday open fencing.周三 Open Fence 后半段状态。
  • Saturday morning readiness.周六上午启动速度。
  • Whether the first bout still starts slowly.比赛第一场是否仍然慢热。
  • Whether legs feel chronically heavy.腿部是否长期沉重。

Adjustment Rule / 调整规则

  • If legs stay heavy, explosiveness drops, or focus declines, reduce morning activation first.如果持续腿重、爆发下降、注意力差，第一优先是减少晨间刺激量。
  • Do not automatically add running volume when performance feels poor.不要因为状态差就自动增加跑量。
  • Recovery quality matters more than simply adding volume.恢复质量比堆训练量更重要。
